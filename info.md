# 🛡️ Desi Alarm & Smart Lock

Control your **Desi Alarm** systems and **Smart Locks** directly from Home Assistant. This integration provides a seamless connection to secure your home.

## 🚀 Key Features
* **Full Alarm Control:** Arm Away, Arm Home, and Disarm your system.
* **Smart Locking:** Lock and unlock your Desi smart doors with real-time status updates.
* **Service Support:** Trigger specific Desi features via Home Assistant services.
* **Config Flow:** Easy setup through the UI (No YAML required!).

## 🛠️ Requirements
- An active **Desi Cloud** account.
- Your Desi devices must be online and connected to the internet.

## ⚙️ How to Setup
1. After downloading this integration via HACS, **restart** Home Assistant.
2. Go to **Settings** -> **Devices & Services**.
3. Click **Add Integration** and search for **Desi**.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to log in.
